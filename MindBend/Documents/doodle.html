Introduction

 

Doodle Jump !!

Build a bot to reach the top before your opponent does, climbing all the stairs and hurdles on your way.

BOT SPECIFICATIONS:

  • Before the start of match, the bot should fix within a cube of side dimensions 500mm. The bot can stretch with no limits in any direction after the start of match.
  • Maximum allowed voltage is 24V.
  • For pneumatics/hydraulics maximum allowed pressure is 10bar.

    • ARENA

  • The arena will have major hurdles in form of different-sized stairs and other obstacles as illustrated.
  • Details for arena are given in the image.
  • Note: Stair sizes may change unlike shown in figure but will never exceed the given dimensions.
  • There will be various checkpoints and hurdles in the track which will be disclosed on the spot.

    • RULES

  • Each team will be given a single run with no time limit. The team reaching the top with maximum points wins.
  • Each team will be given 5 minutes of trial run before the match to get their bot acquainted with the arena.
  • A team may skip any one obstacle excluding the final obstacle through the match.
  • Team will be allowed to touch the bot or take restart only three times during final run with a penalty of 30 points each. The bot must restart from the last crossed checkpoint in such case.
  • Maximum time allowed for an obstacle is 2 minutes, which otherwise leads to disqualification.
  • Final score = (total score) – time taken(in seconds)/3
  • 50 points to cross the major obstacles, 30 points to cross the minor obstacles and 200 points for finishing the match.
  • 200 bonus points if bot is wireless.
  • A team of maximum 5 members can be formed.
