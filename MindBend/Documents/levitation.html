Introduction

 

Levitacion!!

Build a bot that shoots up the puck in its opponent’s goal before the time rides out. The
team to make first 7 shots in opponent’s goal qualifies.

BOT SPECIFICATIONS:

  • Bot sizes should not exceed 350mm X 250mm.
  • No restrictions for height and weight.
  • Bot can be wired or wireless with a compulsory striking mechanism.

    • ARENA

  • Standard air hockey table of approximate dimensions of 300cm X 125cm (length x breadth) will be made available.
  • Standard puck of diameter 6.5cm.

    • RULES and SCORING

  • A coin toss decides which team gets first possession of the puck.
  • For each puck shot in opponent’s goal, a point-score will be recorded.
  • The first team to score 7 points by shooting the puck into the opponent's goal wins the game OR the team with maximum points in the game run of 5 minutes wins.
  • Once the puck is on a certain team's side of the center line, team has 10 seconds to hit the puck back across the center line. Otherwise a foul is recorded and opponent team receives possession of the puck.
  • Any team member cannot touch or strike the puck with any part of his/her body or with any object other than the bot. Doing so records a foul and possession changes hands.
  • Hitting the puck when it is on opposite side of the center line, or crossing the center line COMPLETELY with one's bot causes a foul. Here, the opponent team receives possession of the puck.
  • If the puck leaves the table, a foul is called on the team that caused the puck to go out of play due to offensive motion and the opposing team gets possession of the puck.
  • No team can hold the puck for more than 3 seconds. Doing so causes a foul and possession changes.
  • The time measured by the organizers will be final and used for scoring the teams.
  • Time measured by any contestant by any other means is not acceptable for scoring.
  • In case of any dispute or ambiguity, event coordinators’ decision will be final.
  • Elimination on knock-out basis.
  • A team of maximum 4 members can be formed.
