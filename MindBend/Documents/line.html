Introduction

 

Line stalker !!

Build an autonomous bot reaching the finish point of given track with multiple branches following shortest path in minimum time.

BOT SPECIFICATIONS:

  • Bot sizes should not exceed 250mm X 250mm.
  • Maximum allowed voltage is 24V.
  • The bot must use only on-board power supply. No external off-board power supply is allowed.
  • The bot must be fully autonomous with all powering and motoring mechanisms self-contained.

    • ARENA

  • For qualifying round, complete track will be white in color with black base.
  • The track will be a blend of varieties for obstacles like curves, sharp turns, a 25-degree slope, discontinuity in track (up to 5cm), change of track color (only once; black track on white base), multiple branching of track at the end with only one branch leading to the finish.
  • A snap of sample track showing all hurdles is shown here. (Note: This is not the actual track and the order of hurdles may change.)

    • RULES

  • Qualifying round will be of 5 minutes with limited hurdles.
  • Before the match, each team will be allowed a 15 minute
    • of calibration time on track. Hence the time for pre-game play should be used wisely and judiciously.
  • Maximum time limit is 7 minutes.
  • The team to reach finish-point in lowest time wins.
  • In case the bot is stuck at some point a restart will be allowed only once with a penalty of 30 seconds. The bot will have to start from the beginning. The clock will not be restarted.
  • The time measured by the organizers will be final and used for scoring.
  • Time measured by any contestant by any other means is not acceptable for scoring.
  • In case of any dispute or ambiguity, event coordinators’ decision will be final.
  • Further details will be updated soon.
