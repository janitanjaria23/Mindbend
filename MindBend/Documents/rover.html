Introduction

 

Rover Reconnoiter !!

Build a robust manual bot that can outlive the rocky, topsy-turvy terrain. Accept the challenge, strike the crags, endure the obstacles and reach the top.

BOT SPECIFICATIONS:

  • max size 350X300(length x breadth).
  • no restrictions on height n weight.
  • Max voltage- 24V.

    • RULES

  • Qualifying round will be fun with limited difficulties and 7 minutes’ time on track. The qualifying teams will move on to the final round. The final round will be more exciting with new and more complex obstacles.
  • No practicing on tracks will be allowed.
  • Team will be allowed to touch the bot only three times with a penalty of 15 seconds each.
  • The bot should not touch guarding boundaries of the tracks otherwise a penalty of 5 seconds will be imposed.
  • Final score = (total score) – time(in seconds)/3
  • 50 points to cross the major obstacles, 25 points to cross the minor obstacles and 250 points for finishing the race.
  • 100 bonus points if the bot is wireless.
  • Maximum time allowed for an obstacle is 2 minutes which otherwise leads to disqualification.
  • Team may skip any two obstacles excluding the final obstacle.
  • The bot with on-board battery gets 50 points extra.
  • No repairing time will be allowed.
  • Maximum of 4 members per team are allowed with only 2 members to operate the bot on track.
  • In case of any dispute or ambiguity, event coordinators’ decision will be final.
