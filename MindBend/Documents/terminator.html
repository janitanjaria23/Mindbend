Introduction

 

Terminator 1.0 !!

Design and construct a remote controlled robot capable of fighting a one on one tournament.

BOT SPECIFICATIONS:

  • Bot sizes should not exceed 600mm x 600mm x 1000mm.
  • Maximum allowed weight is 45kg including weight of batteries if off-board.
  • Weight of bot to be counted as 0.7 times of its actual weight if wireless and 1.5 times in case of using external (off-board) pneumatic/hydraulic storage tank.
  • In case of onboard pneumatic/hydraulic storage tank actual weight of bot to be considered including batteries.
  • Maximum allowed pressure for pneumatic/hydraulic cylinder is 10 bar.
  • Maximum allowed voltage is 36V with no external power supply.
  • Restricted: Inflammable chemicals (subject to change in future)
  • Further details will be updated soon.

    • RULES

  • Each fight of 5 minutes.
  • Elimination on knock-out basis.
  • A team loses if bot cannot show sufficient mobility for 30 seconds.
  • A team of maximum 5 members can be formed.
  • In case of any dispute or ambiguity, event coordinators’ decision will be final.
