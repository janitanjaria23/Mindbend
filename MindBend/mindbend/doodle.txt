<h2 class="style7">RULES </h2>
	<div class="pane">
		<p><li> Each team will be given a single run with no time limit. The team reaching the
top with maximum points wins.</li> 
<li>  Each team will be given 3 minutes of trial run before the match to get their bot
acquainted with the arena.</li> 
 
<li> Team will be allowed to touch the bot or take restart only three times during
final run with a penalty of 30 points each. The bot must restart from the last
crossed checkpoint in such case.</li> 
<li> Maximum time allowed for an obstacle is 2 minutes, which otherwise leads to
disqualification.</li> 

<li> Final score = (total score) – time taken(in seconds)/3</li> 

<li> 50 points to cross the major obstacles, 30 points to cross the minor obstacles
and 200 points for finishing the match.</li> 

<li> 200 bonus points if bot is wireless.</li> 

<li> A team of maximum 5 members can be formed.</li> 
</p>
