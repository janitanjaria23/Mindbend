<h2 class="style6"><strong>Problem Statement:</strong></h2>
	<div class="pane">
		<li class="style8">1.Participants are required to present their idea of an eco friendly house in the form of a presentation with a model showing their ideas by Integrating the best ancient practices with modern technology.</li>
	</div>



	<h2 class="style7">Rules</h2>
	<div class="pane">
	
		<li class="style8">1.	Team can comprise of maximum 3 members.</li>
		<li class="style8">2.	The event consists of two phases: </li>
         <li class="style8">i.	The online abstract submission </li>
 		<li class="style8">ii.	Presentation by the shortlisted candidates on the basis of the abstract submitted.                 </li>
		<li class="style8">3.	The final selected teams will present their idea during MindBend.</li>
		<li class="style8">4.	The shortlisted teams will be notified via e-mail.</li>
		<li class="style8">5.	The winners will be selected by a team of judges based on their presentation (they are supposed to clear any queries of the judges or other participants raised during the presentation).</li>
		<li class="style8">6.	The decision of judges will be final and binding.</li>
		<li class="style8">7.	Interdisciplinary participation is allowed.</li>
	
	</div>

	
<h2 class="style7">Specifications:</h2>
	<div class="pane">
		<p>
		
		<li class="style8">1.	Registration fee is Rs. 60/- per team.</li>
<li class="style8">2.	The abstract should contain team name, members names, Department name and Institute name, and should not be more than 200 words.
</li>
<li class="style8">3.	An abstract should give a basic idea of the house. Images can also be included but it should be original and developed by the team using any software.</li>
<li class="style8">4.	An abstract needs to be submitted electronically to the MindBend website on or before 20 September 2012.</li>
<li class="style8">5.	The participants are also required to give a basic 3D idea of the house by presenting a model and a ppt ( power point presentation) on the same. It should clearly mention your proposed ideas and design. </li>
<li class="style8"> Following points should be kept in mind regarding the model:</li>
<li class="style8">i.	The house should be built within the specified area of 2500 sq cm.</li>
<li class="style8">ii.	No materials will be provided by the organizers, participants can use materials of their own choice.</li>



</p>
