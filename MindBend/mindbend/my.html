MY TOUCH SCREEN!!

  • My TouchSCREEN is a workshop based on development of computing systems based on human touch and behaviour. The idea behind the workshop is to develop an interest among the upcoming engineers by giving them knowledge of various type of electronics based touchscreen systems with their basic building along with their integration with fifth generation of computers, we say as Artificial Intelligence..

    • Workshop Detail

  • This workshop will be given by EI systems which is renown group for organizing workshops.
  • It will include a kit of touchscreen which is worth of 2500 rupees.
  • Fees for students attending this workshop will be 750 rupees .
  • Certificate of participation will be given to each student attending workshop.

    • Duration

  • This workshop’s duration is 2 full days.
