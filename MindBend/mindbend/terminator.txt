<h2 class="style6"><strong>BOT SPECIFICATIONS:</strong></h2>
	<div class="pane">
		<p><li> Bot should fix within a box of 60x60x100 cm before the start of the match</li>
<li>Maximum allowed weight is 45kg including weight of batteries if off-board.</li>
<li>Weight of wireless bot to be counted as 0.7 times of its actual weight and 1.5
times in case of using external (off-board) pneumatic/hydraulic storage tank.(not to count weight of storage tank in such case) </li>
<li>In case of onboard pneumatic/hydraulic storage tank actual weight of bot to be
considered including batteries.</li>
<li>Weight of batteries to be considered in all the cases.</li>
<li>Maximum allowed pressure for pneumatic/hydraulic cylinder is 10 bar.</li>
<li>Maximum allowed voltage is 36V with no external power supply.</li>
<li>Restricted:- Use of inflammable CHEMICALS and HIGH-speed projectiles. </li>
</p>
	</div>



	
	
<h2 class="style7">RULES </h2>
	<div class="pane">
		<p><li>Each fight of 5 minutes.</li>
<li>Elimination on knock-out basis.</li>
<li>A team loses if bot cannot show sufficient mobility for 30 seconds.</li>
<li>The team would be judged on the basis of performance during the fight(attacking and defence). </li>
<li>Before the start of the match the team should disclose their attacking mechanisms to the organisers.</li>
<li>A team of maximum 5 members can be formed.</li>
<li>In case of any dispute or ambiguity, event coordinators decision will be final.</li>
</p>
